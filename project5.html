Loomina

Full stack / Design

Vue, TypeScript, Node, Koa, Supabase, Figma

Live site

Loomina is a generative AI platform for digital storytelling and interactive narratives.

The Process

As the lead product engineer for Loomina, I was tasked with creating an immersive and user-friendly interface for both story creators and readers. Using Vue and TypeScript, I developed a flexible system that allows for the creation and consumption of branching narratives and interactive lessons.


In addition to the technical implementation, I was also responsible for the overall branding and visual design of Loomina. Using Figma, I created a cohesive design system that reflects the platform's focus on creativity and learning.
