The Process

My role at myuser is to design and build the frontend of the product from start to finish. Using Vue and TypeScript for the webapp, and HTMX for the marketing site, the goal is for users of all kinds and organisations of all sizes to have an intuitive and extensive CRM.

I also build the design system for the webapp, and created the logo and branding for the business as a whole using Figma.
