Annabel Curphey portfolio

Front End / Design

Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Live site

This website was built for a client named Annabel Curphey, a songwriter based in Brighton, UK.

The Process

The project begun with a conversation, speaking about what annabel needed the content of the site to contain as well as where she wanted to go stylistically with the design. In the end, we opted for a cute and simplistic layout with a salmon pink and rgb(12, 12, 12); color scheme to display her light hearted and easy going temperement.


With a hero section, a bio, imbeded video and a contact email, this simple website serves the purpose of introducing Annabel as a songwriter and performer as clearly and simplistically as possible.
