Dismiss Yourself

Front End / Design

Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue.js, Sanity

Live site

This website is a home for the record label Dismiss Yourself, built within a small team of designers / developers.

The Process

The aim of this site was to establish a home for the label, bringing their digital presence into one place. Simplicity was the goal here, keeping the current branding in mind whilst maintaining core UI/UX principles to make the user experience clean and efficient.

My role in this project consisted of working as part of a team to design the home and releases section as well as making some design decisions with their shopify store and providing front-end related technical support to other team members.
